# ADR-NNN: Title

- **Status:** Proposed | Accepted | Superseded by ADR-XXX
- **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
- **Deciders:** names/roles

## Context

What situation, constraint, or evidence forces a decision? State the problem
without presupposing the answer.

## Decision

The decision, stated as a full sentence in the active voice.

## Alternatives considered

Each realistic alternative and the concrete reason it was not chosen.

## Consequences

What becomes easier, what becomes harder, and what new obligations exist.
Include follow-up work the decision creates.

## Security and operations impact

Effects on tenant isolation, authentication, data protection, deployment,
backups, or observability. Write "none identified" only after considering it.

## Reconsideration triggers

Concrete conditions under which this decision must be revisited. An ADR
without triggers is dogma.
